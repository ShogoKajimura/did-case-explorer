Restricted intake workflow

Submit a paper for OCR, bibliographic triage, and later review.

This page is the public-facing intake surface for scanned or hard-to-access DID/MPD case literature. Submitted PDFs are processed on a restricted backend and are not made public through this site by default.

What happens after upload

The restricted backend stores the PDF privately, creates a job record, generates a searchable PDF, and extracts canonical OCR text. After submission, this site provides a private status page for progress tracking and artifact download. Public release, if any, is handled separately and conservatively.

What this page does not do

It does not publish your uploaded file, promise immediate human review, or expose copyrighted full text through the public explorer.

Contributor credit

Names are shown publicly only if you opt in. If you provide a homepage, lab page, or ORCID URL, the directory can link your name out from the top section of the explorer.

Submission intake is separated from the public metadata explorer. Public release and restricted full-text handling are intentionally decoupled.
